Transportation

Transportation is major in the state of Washington. Transport is vital because it enables communication, trade and other forms of exchange between people that turn establishes civilizations. Transport plays an important part in economic growth and globalization, but most types cause air pollution and use large amounts of land. There are multiple transportation systems in Washington such as:

  • State Routes
  • Ferry Systems
  • Public and Private Airports
  • Freight Railroads
  • Sound Transit Link Light Rail
Washington State Department of Transportation (WSDOT) is constantly looking for ways to shorten commute times in Washington. As public transportation is one of the most common ways people transport between destinations. WSDOT are constantly working on new projects to implement new transportation ideas and update transportation systems in various counties.

Tourist attractions in Washington

Space Needle

The Space Needle is one of the most popular buildings in Seattle Washington and the top tourist destination. This bulding is 605 feet tall and allows you to view the city in a 360 degree view. The space needle was built April 17, 1961 and since its opening nearly 60 million people have visted the Space Needle making it one of the top tourist attarctions. On April 19, 1999 the Space Needle was officially considered a landmark according to the cities landmarks preservation board.

Mount Rainier

Mount Rainier is mountain located in the cascade range of the Pacific northwest. This mountain is 14,411 feet tall allowing hikers with breath taking views. Mount Rainier is considered a national park which was established in 1899. This mountain also protects the headwaters of five major watersheds orginated in the mountains glaciers making it an important source of water for the Puget Sound. It's not only a great tourist spot with great views, hikig trails, waterfalls. It's also an important source of water for the Puget Sound.

Pikes Place Market

Pikes Place Market has been open for over 108 years and is one of the most popular tourist spots in Seattle. It is considered one of the oldest continuously running markets in the country. Anually over 10 million people visit this tourist attraction. This market has many diffrent spots making it overall fun for tourist to visit such as the gum wall, the orginal starbucks, Marvels fresh flowers, Rachel the pig statue as well as the amazing views overall pikes place has so much enertainment to offer.

Chihuly Garden and Glass

Chihuly Garden and Glass is a exhibit showcasing Dale Chihuly's studio glass, This exhibit has been open since May 31, 2012. This is a highly visited tourist attraction as it has many interesting things to offer beside Dale Chihuly's amazing work there is a cafe, theater, bookstore, chandelier walk, garden and a glass house. All of these attractions are held on 1.5 acres of land. Overall this space allows for there to be many attractions and makes it apealing to a tourist to visit the tour can last up 2 hours.
